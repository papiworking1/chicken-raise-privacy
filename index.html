Last Updated: September 28, 2025

Welcome to Chicky Ways! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Game Data: Your game progress, scores, and achievements
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the game, session duration, feature usage
  • Technical Data: Crash reports, performance data, and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the gaming experience
  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Analyze game performance and fix technical issues
  • Send push notifications (with your consent)
  • Understand user behavior to enhance gameplay

3. Third-Party Services

Our game may use third-party services for analytics and advertising:

  • AppsFlyer: For attribution and analytics

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your information
  • Delete your game data
  • Opt-out of push notifications
  • Request information about data we collect

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
